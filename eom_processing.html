⚙ What this procedure does

  • 1Finds every loan with status Auto-Approved.
  • 2Calculates each loan's monthly installment as Loan Amount ÷ Duration.
  • 3Deducts the installment from the customer's oldest Active account that has sufficient balance.
  • 4If no active account can cover the installment, the loan is marked Defaulted.
  • 5Returns a summary of how many payments succeeded and how many defaulted.
⚠ This action is irreversible. Real funds are deducted from customer accounts and loans may be marked as Defaulted. Only run this once per month at end-of-period.
